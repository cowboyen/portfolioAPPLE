Overview

Designed and developed a comprehensive My Account solution for Fagmøbler, Norway's leading furniture retailer. The project focused on creating an intuitive and user-friendly account management system based on extensive UX research and best practices.

Methodology

The UX design process was firmly grounded in Baymard Institute's research and best practices,ensuring a data-driven approach to user experience. All design decisions were meticulously documented to facilitate seamless handover to UI designers and maintain design consistency throughout the project.

Design process

Initial ideation and wireframing were conducted in FigJam, allowing for rapid iteration and stakeholder feedback. This collaborative approach enabled early validation of concepts and user flows. Following stakeholder approval, the design process moved to Figma where detailed UX documentation was integrated alongside the design work.

Low-fi prototypes of my account

My account features

The My Account solution includes six key features:

  • My orders: View all orders, track active orders and download receipts
  • Favorites Save and organize favorite products
  • My profile: Update personal information and manage security settings
  • Privacy and notifications: Control marketing preferences and communication settings
  • Payment methods Store and manage payment cards
  • Customer service Quick access to the help center that provdes FAQ and contact information

Implementation

Design specifications and UX patterns were thoroughly documented, providing clear direction for the UI design phase while maintaining flexibility for visual design decisions. The extensive documentation ensures design consistency, best practice and facilitates future updates and maintenance.

Impact

The resulting My Account system provides Fagmøbler's customers with a professional, secure, and user-friendly platform for managing their purchases and account information. The implementation of Baymard Institute's best practices ensures a high-quality user experience that meets modern e-commerce standards.
